Bg 6.9
TEXT 9
TEXT
suhrn-mitrary-udasina-
madhyastha-dvesya-bandhusu
sadhusv api ca papesu
sama-buddhir visisyate
SYNONYMS
suhrt-by nature a well-wisher; mitra-benefactor with affection; ari-enemy; udasina-neutral between the belligerents; madhyastha-mediator between the belligerents; dvesya-envious; bandhusu-among the relatives or well-wishers; sadhusu-unto the pious; api-as well as; ca-and; papesu-unto the sinners; sama-buddhih-having equal intelligence; visisyate-is far advanced.
TRANSLATION
A person is said to be still further advanced when he regards all-the honest well-wisher, friends and enemies, the envious, the pious, the sinner and those who are indifferent and impartial-with an equal mind.
